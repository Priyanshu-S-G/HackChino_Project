Our Mission

To make brain tumor diagnosis easy and accessible in the rural areas where expert radiologists are not easily available to make sure every one can receive treatment before its too late.Since manual MRI segmentation is quite time-cosuming, error-prone and less consistent, we want to provide everyone the same opportunity of better medical facility regarless of their location of residence.

How can we help you?

We use an AI model which classifies and segments brain tumors from MRI scans using deep learning. This makes the diagnosis easily available to everyone and ensures it is cost effective and error free.
