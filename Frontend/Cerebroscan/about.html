About Us

At CerebroScan, we are driven by a mission to revolutionize brain tumor diagnosis through the power of artificial intelligence. Access to expert radiological care shouldn't be limited by geography or resources.
That’s why we’ve developed a lightweight, AI-powered web application that empowers healthcare providers—especially in rural and under-resourced areas—with fast, reliable brain tumor classification and segmentation from MRI scans.
Our platform supports multiple tumor types, including glioma, meningioma, and pituitary tumors. Designed for local/offline deployment with Docker, CerebroScan ensures portability, privacy, and ease of use even without internet access.
We combine technical excellence with a deep commitment to healthcare accessibility, aiming to assist doctors and clinics in making accurate, timely diagnoses—anytime, anywhere.
